## Description

The TAC 4 gives you four independent control points on a single switch face — for rooms where every circuit matters and every detail is considered.

---

## Technical Overview

A 4-button Zigbee smart switch with independent load control or scene triggering per button. Backlit with customizable icons. Available in PP or anti-glare glass in black, white, or gold.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 4 (independent)
- Relay: 10A total (shared)
- Direct Load: Up to 2A per load
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Materials: PP or AG glass
- Colours: Black, White, Gold

⚠ 10A total shared relay capacity. For loads above 2A use an external SSR or contactor.
