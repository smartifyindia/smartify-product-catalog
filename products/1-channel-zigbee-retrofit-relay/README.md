## Technical Overview

A single-channel Zigbee switching relay designed to retrofit any standard mechanical switch into a smart switch — no rewiring, no hardware replacement.

Installs behind the existing wall switch in a flush box or on DIN rail. Supports direct load switching up to 4A; for loads between 4A and 10A, an external relay, SSR, or contactor is required. Communicates natively over Zigbee 3.0 with full Home Assistant support, and integrates with Matter and Apple HomeKit via a compatible hub.

Key Specifications
- Channels: 1
- Max Switching: 10A
- Direct Load: Up to 4A
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Compatible with: Home Assistant (native), Matter & Apple HomeKit (via hub)
- Installation: Flush box or DIN rail

⚠ For loads above 4A, an external relay, SSR, or contactor is required.
