## Technical Overview

A 4-button Zigbee touch panel with two dedicated fan control channels for ceiling fans up to 120W each, providing load switching, scene control, and dual fan management from a single wall panel.

Button channels are independently configurable for toggle or momentary operation. Available in white or black glass with silver, gold, or black bezel.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 4 capacitive touch
- Fan Channels: 2 (up to 120W each)
- Relay: 10A per button channel
- Direct Load: Up to 4A per button channel
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Installation: Wall-mounted

⚠ For loads above 4A on button channels, use an external SSR or contactor.
