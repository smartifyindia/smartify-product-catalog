## Technical Overview

A dual-channel Zigbee switching relay that converts two standard mechanical switches into smart switches simultaneously — without replacing any existing hardware.

Each channel operates independently with direct load switching up to 4A. For loads above 4A per channel, an external relay, SSR, or contactor is required. Installs behind existing switches in a flush box or on DIN rail. Communicates natively over Zigbee 3.0 with Home Assistant, and integrates with Matter and Apple HomeKit via a compatible hub.

Key Specifications
- Channels: 2 (independent)
- Max Switching: 5A per channel
- Direct Load: Up to 4A per channel
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Compatible with: Home Assistant (native), Matter & Apple HomeKit (via hub)
- Installation: Flush box or DIN rail

⚠ Direct load switching only — not a dry contact output and not a dimmer.
