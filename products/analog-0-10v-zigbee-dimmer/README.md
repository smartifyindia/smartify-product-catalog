## Technical Overview

A single-channel Zigbee 0-10V analog dimming controller designed to integrate with LED drivers and lighting systems that accept a 0-10V dimming input signal.

Outputs a low-voltage 0-10V control signal — it does not switch or dim mains voltage directly. Designed for DIN rail or panel installation. Communicates natively over Zigbee 3.0 with Home Assistant, and integrates with Matter and Apple HomeKit via a compatible hub.

Key Specifications
- Channels: 1
- Output: 0-10V analog control signal
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Compatible with: Home Assistant (native), Matter & Apple HomeKit (via hub)
- Installation: DIN rail / electrical panel

⚠ Control signal output only — does not switch or dim high-voltage loads directly. Compatible 0-10V LED drivers required.
