## Technical Overview

A 10-button Zigbee capacitive touch panel for comprehensive load switching and scene control across up to 10 independent channels in residential and commercial installations.

Each button independently controls a load or triggers a scene. Configurable for toggle or momentary operation per button. Available in white or black glass with silver, gold, or black bezel and configurable backlight.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 10 capacitive touch
- Relay: 10A per channel
- Direct Load: Up to 4A per channel
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Finish: White/Black glass + Silver/Gold/Black bezel
- Installation: Wall-mounted

⚠ For loads above 4A, use an external SSR or contactor.
