## Description

Load control and scenes. One switch.

The TAC 6+ combines the practical with the experiential — four buttons that control circuits, two that set the scene. Everything your room needs, on a single surface.

---

## Technical Overview

A 6-button Zigbee smart switch with 4 load control buttons and 2 dedicated scene trigger buttons. Combines direct load switching with scene control on a single switch face.

The 4 load buttons share a 10A relay capacity; the 2 scene buttons contain no relay and trigger automations only. Available in PP or anti-glare glass in black, white, or gold.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 4 load + 2 scene
- Relay: 10A total (4 load buttons, shared)
- Direct Load: Up to 2A per load
- Scene Buttons: Trigger only (no relay)
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Materials: PP or AG glass
- Colours: Black, White, Gold

⚠ 10A total shared relay capacity across load buttons. For loads above 2A use external SSR or contactor.
