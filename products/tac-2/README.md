## Description

Two circuits. One switch.

The TAC 2 controls two loads from a single elegant switch face — light and fan, two rooms, or two scenes. Available in glass or polypropylene in the finish you prefer.

---

## Technical Overview

A 2-button Zigbee smart switch with independent load control or scene triggering per button. Backlit with customizable icons. Available in PP or anti-glare glass in black, white, or gold.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 2 (independent)
- Relay: 10A total (shared)
- Direct Load: Up to 4A per load
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Materials: PP or AG glass
- Colours: Black, White, Gold

⚠ 10A total shared relay capacity. For loads above 4A use an external SSR or contactor.
