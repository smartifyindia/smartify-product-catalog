## Technical Overview

A battery-powered Zigbee scene switch with 6 configurable buttons for wireless scene and automation triggering — no wiring required.

Can be wall-mounted or surface-mounted at any location without electrical installation. Backlit with customizable icons. Available in PP or anti-glare glass in black, white, or gold.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 6
- Power: Battery
- Function: Scene triggers only (no relay)
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Materials: PP or AG glass
- Colours: Black, White, Gold
- Installation: Wireless, wall or surface mount

⚠ No relay — does not switch electrical loads. Battery-powered scene control only.
