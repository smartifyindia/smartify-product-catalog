## Description

Three loads. One clean face.

The TAC 3 manages three circuits from a single switch — lighting zones, fans, or scenes — in a finish that makes the hardware disappear into your interior.

---

## Technical Overview

A 3-button Zigbee smart switch with independent load control or scene triggering per button. Backlit with customizable icons. Available in PP or anti-glare glass in black, white, or gold.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 3 (independent)
- Relay: 10A total (shared)
- Direct Load: Up to 3A per load
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Materials: PP or AG glass
- Colours: Black, White, Gold

⚠ 10A total shared relay capacity. For loads above 3A use an external SSR or contactor.
